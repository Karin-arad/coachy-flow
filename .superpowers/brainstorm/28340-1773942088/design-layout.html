Key Design Decisions

  • Dot progress instead of rainbow bar - cleaner, less distracting
  • White cards on cream - depth without heavy shadows
  • Large tap targets - minimum 44px touch areas everywhere
  • Rounded corners (16-20px) - warm, friendly feel
  • Orange gradient slider - instead of default browser slider
  • Grid time selection - easier to scan than a list
  • Video thumbnail preview - before committing to play
  • Tags on workout card - quick scan of intensity, duration, type
